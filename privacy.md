# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Overview

GardenOS respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## What We Collect

### Website
This website does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

### GardenOS App
GardenOS stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to:
- Your plant information
- Your photos
- Your journal entries
- Any personal data

If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs through your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
