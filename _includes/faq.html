Frequently Asked Questions

When will GardenOS launch?

We're targeting early 2026 for iOS, mid 2026 for iPad. Sign up below to be notified the day we launch.

What platforms are supported?

iOS first (early 2026), followed by iPadOS (mid 2026). We want to get the iPhone experience right before expanding to iPad.

Is my data really private?

Completely. GardenOS stores everything locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs through your personal iCloud account - we never see it. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection.

Why only 3 plants on the free plan?

We want GardenOS to be sustainable without ads or data mining. The free plan lets you try the app fully, and the Pro subscription keeps development going.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Your garden journal is yours. We'll support standard export formats so you're never locked in.

Will there be an Android version?

Not immediately, but it's on our radar for the future. We want to do iOS really well first.

Who builds GardenOS?

GardenOS is built by Hayeshi, a small software company in Colombia focused on privacy-respecting indie apps.
